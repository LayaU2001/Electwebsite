Product Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my new device?

Most of our products come with a quick start guide. You can also find detailed setup instructions in the product manual available on our website. For visual guidance, check out our YouTube channel with step-by-step setup videos.

What's included in the warranty?

Our standard warranty covers manufacturing defects for one year from the date of purchase. This includes parts and labor for repairs. The warranty doesn't cover accidental damage or normal wear and tear. You can extend your warranty up to 3 years at checkout.

How do I update my device firmware?

Firmware updates are typically delivered through our companion app. Open the app, go to Device Settings, and check for updates. Make sure your device is charged or plugged in during the update process.

Where can I find replacement parts?

Replacement parts are available in the Accessories section of our website. If you can't find what you're looking for, contact our support team with your device model number and we'll help locate the right parts for you.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team 24/7 through the live chat on this page, by email at support@techsolutions.com, or by phone at 1-800-TECH-HELP. Average response time is under 15 minutes for chat and email.
